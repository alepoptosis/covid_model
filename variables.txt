name                    access  description

age                     ALL     the age range of the person (0-29, 30-59, 60+)
asym-infections         G       the percentage of asymptomatic symptomatics
closed-system?          G       whether outside infections can occur
contact-list            LI      list of susceptibles the agent interacted with
control-measures?       G       whether add control measures after 1st lockdown
control-threshold       G       percentage of symptomatics to trigger mask use
control-threshold-num   G       number of symptomatics to trigger mask use
currently-locked?       G       whether a lockdown is in progress
death-countdown         IA      individual death countdown
death-mean              G       mean of the normal dist for death
death-stdev             G       stdev of the normal dist for death
duration                G       length of simulation (years)
first-lockdown?         G       whether first lockdown occurred
imm-countdown           R       individual immunity period
immunity-mean           G       mean of the poisson dist for immunity
imposed-lockdown?       G       whether to simulate gov-imposed lockdown
inc-countdown           L       individual incubation countdown
incubation-mean         G       mean of the log-normal dist for incubation
incubation-stdev        G       stdev of the log-normal dist for incubation
initial-inf             G       number of symptomatics at start of simulation
iso-countdown           I       individual isolation countdown
iso-countdown-max       G       maximum isolation period for symptomatics
isolate-symptomatics?   G       whether symptomatics should be asked to isolate
isolate-threshold       G       percentage of symptomatics to trigger isolation
isolate-threshold-num   G       number of symptomatics to trigger isolation
isolation-strictness    G       the percentage of people isolating for max days
lockdown-strictness     G       likelihood a person will obey lockdown
lockdown-threshold      G       percentage of symptomatics to trigger lockdown
lockdown-threshold-num  G       number of symptomatics to trigger lockdown
mean-iso-reduction      G       mean of the poisson dist for isolation reduction
num-contacts            G       the number of contacts occurring at that tick
p-death                 G       probability of dying instead of recovering
p-infect                S       individual transmission probability
p-infect-init           G       base transmission probability
pop-size                G       the number of agents in the simulation
protection-strength     G       strength of measures to reduce transmission
rec-countdown           IA      individual recovery countdown
recovery-mean           G       mean of the normal dist for recovery
recovery-stdev          G       stdev of the normal dist for recovery
start-isolation?        G       whether isolation of symptomatics should start
test-and-trace?         G       whether the test and trace system is in place
test-coverage           G       percentage of symptomatic cases tested
testtrace-threshold     G       number of symptomatics to start testing (TBA)
to-become-infected?     L       flags a L for beginning of infection
to-become-latent?       S       flags a S for exposure
to-become-susceptible?  R       flags a R for loss of immunity
to-remove               IA      flags an I/A for removal (recovery or death)
travel-strictness       G       probability of outside infections occurring
will-die?               IA      whether the infected will die or recover
z-contact               ALL     individual radius of contact neighbourhood
z-contact-init          ALL     base radius of contact neighbourhood
z-contact-min           G       the minimum radius of contact neighbourhoods
